# Vibe Work Session Templates

Use these templates only when they help the session move faster.

## Starter Prompt

```text
I am starting a Vibe Work: Ship One Thing Only session.

Help me:
1. Identify the one challenge or opportunity worth focusing on now.
2. Define what "shipped" means for this session.
3. Generate 2-3 practical options.
4. Recommend the best path.
5. Pause for my choice at important decision points.
6. Produce the actual artifact, plan, message, decision, or next action before the session ends.

Time available:
People present:
Current context:
```

## Human Invite

```text
Want to do a quick Vibe Work session with me?

The goal is to ship one concrete thing, not boil the ocean.

Focus:
Time box:
Your role:
What we should leave with:

I will use AI to help structure the session, generate options, and turn our conversation into the actual output.
```

## 25-Minute Agenda

```text
0:00-0:04  Arrive and choose the one thing
0:04-0:07  Define shipped and constraints
0:07-0:12  Explore options and choose path
0:12-0:21  Produce the artifact
0:21-0:24  Assign owners and dates
0:24-0:25  Capture learning and parked topics
```
